Spinach Tortellini with Sweet Chicken and Apple Sausage

Spinach Tortellini from Trader Joe's goes super will with the Sweet Chicken and Apple sausage from there. Add some butter, olive oil and lemon juice and you have a full on, really simple meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of TJ's Spinach Tortellini
  • 2 sausages (you dont have to use the sweet apple ones, but I think they go great here)
  • Spinach and arugula, really any salad greens you like
  • Butter
  • Olive oil (not cooking oil)
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Spinach tortellini as per packaged instructions
  2. Cut sausage into bite sized pieces (same as tortellini)
  3. Cook sausage in medium skillet until browned
  4. Drain tortellini on top of preferred salad greens in collander
  5. Mix tortellini, salad greens and sausage in same pot used for tortellini
  6. Add butter, olive oil, lemon juice and SnP to taste. Serve with parmesan!
